HOW TO MAKE OATMEAL BY DARIUS

Description

Oatmeal is a very common breakfast across many cultures. Although some people use milk instead of water, almost nobody has oatmeal raw. There are many different flavors such as brown sugar, apple, and many more.

Ingredients
  • Instant Oatmeal
  • Hot Water
Steps
  1. Put a reasonable amount of oatmeal in a bowl
  2. Put a reasonable amount of hot water in the same bowl
