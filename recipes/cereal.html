HOW TO MAKE CEREAL BY DARIUS

Description

Cereal is a very common breakfast across all cultures. Although some people have it at night, most people have it early in the morning. Some varieties include Cheerios, Frosted Flakes, Mini Wheats, and Fruit Loops.

Ingredients
  • Milk
  • Cereal
Steps
  1. Put a reasonable amount of cereal in a bowl
  2. Put a reasonable amount of milk in the same bowl
